Start Here

Add a Course

Save courses to your account. We offer the following courses: Contracts, Torts, Real Property, and Criminal Law. You can add and save the classes you want to your account.

Read Materials

Search Cases

Search our massive case law database for cases from your syllabus. You can save every case you want to your course. If you save a case you can read and brief the case.

Browse Codes

Browse our selection of Codes and Statutes. Codes are organized by book and section. Codes can be added to courses and read just like cases.

Add Custom Materials

Can't find what you're looking for in our database? You can add your own opinion, statute, regulation, or code. After you add the material you can read and brief it.

Take Notes

Take Notes


For every material you save to your course you can take notes while you read. Select the text you want to note and save it as a:

  • Fact
  • Issue
  • Rule
  • Holding
  • Analysis
  • Note

Edit Briefs


On the Brief screen you can review the notes you've taken from your material and edit them in real time. Changes are saved automatically. View your brief whenever you need it.

Review Notes

Edit Outlines


All of your briefs can be automatically assembled into an outline. You can choose the relevant sections you want in the outline. You can organize the briefs however you see fit.

Download Outlines


You can always export your course outlines. We allow you to download your course outlines as a document (.doc) or as a spreadsheet (.csv).
