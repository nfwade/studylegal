Note From Founder

My name is Nathan Wade. I am a third year law student and the founder of Study Legal, LLC. Before I entered law school I worked as a software developer. The golden rule of software development is "don't repeat yourself" (DRY). My first year of law school was painfully repetitive. Instead of studying, most of my time was spent taking, organizing, and transferring notes. In response to the time and opportunity wasted in my first year, I spent my second year creating this software. I hope it saves you money, helps you study, and gives you more time for what's really important - whatever that may be.

Our Mission

To minimize the time, and maximize the effectiveness of legal education.

About

Study Legal provides law students all of the public domain materials their classes require in a format that makes reading, briefing, and outlining, a single step.

Services

Basic Service
Free

User's with Basic Accounts can add/remove classes, add materials to each class, and read the materials online, anywhere.
