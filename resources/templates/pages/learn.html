Add a Course

The first time you login you'll be asked to add a course to your account. Simply click one of the listed Available Courses in the menu. You can now begin adding materials to your course.

To add more courses, or remove courses, click the add courses button on the right side of the course menu. Click the course you want to add. If you want to remove a course, just click the course and confirm.

Find a Case

To find a case click in the case search bar and type the case name. All cases that include your search term in their full name will be returned. The more specific you can be the better. To add the case to your course, just click on the case in the search results.

Add a Code

To add a code to your course materials, click the add code button. A menu will open that allows you to browse the codes we offer by collection, section, and code. Click the code to add it to your course materials.

Add a Custom Material

If you can't find what you're looking for you can always add a custom material to your course. To add a custom material, click the custom material button. Give the material a title, and content. You can add opinions, codes, statutes, regulations, and other materials. Submit the material and it will be added to your course.

Arrange Materials

You can change the order your materials are presented in. To rearrange your materials, just click the material you want to move and drag it to the place you want. you can also rearrange entire briefs in the outline view.

Take Notes While you Read

You can save time by taking notes while you read. To add part of a material to your notes, just highlight the section you want to save and click F (Fact), I (Issue), R (Rule), H (Holding), A (Analysis), or N (Notes)

Edit a Material Brief

Click "Brief" to switch to brief mode. In brief mode you can view and edit the notes you've taken from the material. Here you can reword your notes or add class discussion.

View Course Outlines

Click Outline in the menu bar to go to the Outline page. Here you can view an outline for every course your taking. You can toggle the sections you want included in your outline. You can also rearrange your briefs into a helpful order.

Download Course Outlines

You can also export your outlines for further editing or study. You can download your outline as either a spreadsheet (.csv) or a document (.doc).
